Hamburger

Description

This ain't grandmas hamburger

This hamburger is an umami bomb. The secret to it is marinate it in a mix of soy sauce and worcestershire sauce, & fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sauerkraut
  • Sliced jalapeno
  • Sliced tomato
  • Mustard
  • Kethcup
  • Hamburger bun
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Freeze the hamburger patty if you like your burgers between rare and medium.
  2. Mix the soy sauce, fish sauce, and worcestershire sauce.
  3. Marinate the hamburger patty in the sauce mixture for at least an hour.
  4. Remove the patty from the marinade and season with black pepper
  5. Turn the grill on high cook the burger to your desired doneness.
  6. Place the sauerkraut, sliced jalapenos and cheddar cheese on top of patty.
  7. Toast the bun
  8. Spread the condiments on the bun and place the patty on top
  9. Top with the sliced tomato
