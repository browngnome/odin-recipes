Pumpkin pie

Description

Pumpkin pie is a delicious treat typically enjoyed in the fall. It is spiced with a mix of; allspice, ginger, clove, mace, cinnamon, black pepper, & nutmeg.

Pumpkin pie is best served with a dollop of fresh whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream if you are feeling exceptionally naughty.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin puree
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Light brown sugar
  • Allspice
  • Ginger
  • Clove
  • Mace
  • Cinnamon
  • Black pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Kosher salt
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Egg white
  • Pie crust
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix the spices, and salt in a bowl
  3. Whisk the eggs with brown sugar until they have lightened in color
  4. Mix the heavy cream with the pumpkin puree
  5. Stir together all the ingredients
  6. Line the pie crust with parchment paper and fill it with weights
  7. Bake the crust for about 30 minutes, then remove pie crust and weights
  8. Brush the pie crust with the egg white.
  9. Pour the filling into the crust
  10. Bake the pie until the filling is almost set. It should still have a slight wobble when bumped.
  11. Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool completely before serving.
